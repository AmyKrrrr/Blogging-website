POMODODRO TECHNIQUE
One of the EFFICIENT ways of studying.


We all do study but the question is, how far do we REMEMBER what we study? Most of the students have this problem. So, we found a solution to this problem and it is Pomodoro Technique . Firstly, we will look at what it actually is.


Pomodoro technique is one of the most efficient ways of studying. It lets you study with a free mind and will also help you at being focused on what you study. This is one of the most successful ways of studying. If you use this technique daily while studying you will definitely score better. Pomodoro Technique is not only for study purpose but also it can be used for completing or doing any kind of work.

In this blog we will know what is pomodoro technique? How to use it? What are its disadvantages? And how can you overcome it? So, let’s start!!

This technique was introduced so that the students will not stress themselves too much

Now we will learn how to use it.

For example, you have to study a chapter for tomorrow’s test and want to use pomodoro technique. Here is how to use -->


HOW POMODORO WORKS


1. Start a 25-minute timer on your phone or your watch
2. Completely focus on your work/study for the next 25 minutes
3. Stop once the alarm goes off
4. Take a 5 minutes break
5. Resume studying for another 25 minutes and taking a break for another 5 minutes
6. After 4 cycles, take a 30-minutes break
7. Repeat the process until you have finished the preparation of your test



After this you will not have the same level of focus as you had before while reading the same topic. So, here is a thing you need to understand. You have to change your plans according to the topic you are studying. For example, you have a very big chapter to study and subtopics are also very big, in this case 25 minutes might not be sufficient. So, make it 50 minutes with 10 minutes break.

So, according to the amount of work, you have to change your timing also. Sometimes you can also make it topic to topic, once you finish a topic take a 5 minutes break and then start one more topic.

This is how we use pomodoro method.


WHY SHOULD WE USE POMODORO?


Working non-stop on a task for a long period of time without taking a break is bad for productivity:

• You have to spend a lot of time squatting at your desk, so you quickly become tired.
• Working for a long period of time without breaks is often ineffective because you can’t focus on a single task for long time (It makes you bore or tired).

And, because our concentration levels drop, we either give up, or rush the project, which brings down the quality of our work.

So, what can we do about this?

The answer is - not in how much time we actually need to finish something, but in how we use the time we have at our disposal. And this is where the Pomodoro Method will help you.


DISADVANTAGES OF POMODORO METHOD


In this method there are some distractions too. When you take your 5 minutes break you might check your mobile, which is a mistake. Once you open your mobile you will get carried away and will not study. So, avoid using your mobile. Instead of using your mobile go outside to get some fresh air, or do some exercise or something else.

HERE’S HOW TO USE IT


Back in Pomodoro method we study for 25 minutes and take 5 minutes break, But in Animedoro method we study for 40-60 minutes and take break for 20 minutes in which you watch one episode of anime and after one episode again you study for 40-50 minutes.

But in this method also there are some disadvantages. We all know that anime is very addictive, so if we ice one episode of anime, we will feel like to watch another episode of it. So, to overcome this problem we must control ourselves from watching more. If you can control yourself you can use this method of studying.



So that was all about an efficient way of studying. If you use these methods properly, I guarantee that you will improve in your studies. All you have to do is - strictly follow Pomodoro method and also keep in mind not to get distracted by anything. If you watch anime you may also use Animedoro Method, but it’s up to you. Again, in Animedoro method there are chances you may get carried away. So, I will prefer Pomodoro method.
