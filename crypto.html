CRYPTOCURRENCY: An easy Explaination


Cryptocurrency, we all might have heard about it recently. But do you have a clear idea about what it actually is? No? so read this blog till the end and you will understand everything crystal clearly.
Don’t worry we are not going to use complex words. Everything is going to be in simple English.
So, let’s get started ! ! !


WHAT IS CRYPTOCURRENCY?

Cryptocurrency is simply a digital currency, it does not exist physically. Means, you can’t hold a Bitcoin (one of the cryptocurrencies) in your hands but it has some value in the market. We all know that Bitcoin’s value rapidly increased (it has its own reasons like accessibility, etc.) and that is the reason we wanted to know what it is. But Bitcoin is not the only cryptocurrency available, there are more than 4,000 cryptocurrencies in existence.



WHAT IS THE USE OF IT?

Talking about cryptocurrency’s uses, you can use it just like money. Some companies have their own currency and they call them different names. For example, there is a company that provides goods and services but only exchange them with their currency, let’s call them tokens. Now, how do you get these tokens? Simply by exchanging them with real money. After you exchange you can use them to take goods or services provided by that particular company. Crypto works the same way.


IF MONEY AND CRYPTO ARE FOR THE SAME USE THEN WHY CRYPTO?

You might have this doubt, if money and crypto are for the same use then why crypto. Crypto was introduced so that banks have no connection with the money in cryptocurrency form and not only that but also for transactions from one person to the other. In our daily life we make transactions but we pay for the transaction and it happens under banks surveillance. But when we use crypto, we don’t have to pay extra charges for transaction and also it can’t be traced. Above all it guarantees us strong security.

But, many people invest in Crypto for profit, yes, they invest for profit. Let’s take Bitcoin as example, in 2011 one Bitcoin was worth 1 Dollar and now (2021) one Bit Coin is worth 31,634 Dollars. This is a huge profit and that is the reason everyone is interested in Cryptocurrencies.


There are this many advantages of Cryptocurrency but why few countries ban them? Why do some say they are illegal?
Well, because it comes with some disadvantages.


DISADVANTAGES OF CRYPTO

Here are some disadvantages of Crypto -
• The value of it might drop, yes it not only rises but also drops which sometimes can be a loss.
• If you send coins to a person by mistake you can’t retrieve it back.
• Mining cryptocurrencies is affecting the environment as it takes a lot of electricity.
• As Crypto can’t be traced it is perfect for doing illegal deals like buying drugs on the dark web, etc.


HOW CAN WE INVEST IN CRYPTO?

If you want to invest in crypto, here is how to.

1. Firstly you have to find a plateform for trading, there are many plateforms like - CoinDCX, Coinbase, etc.
2. After you find a plateform you have to make an account.
3. After making an account you have to deposit some money. You have to link your bank account with your crypto account to buy crypto.
4. Pick a coin of your choice to buy and make your first purchase.
5. After you make your first purchase, make sure you keep it safe.




CONCLUTION

Now you have a clear idea about what Crypto is. Let’s summarize everything. Crypto does not exist physically but has certain value in the market. Crypto is just like gold if we buy some when it is cheap and sell when its value is high, we will have some profit. It comes with its own advantages and disadvantages as we discussed earlier.
